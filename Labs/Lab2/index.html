Summary

  1. Playing with our pets can raise serotonin and dopamine levels
  2. Knowing that your floppy-eared companion is waiting for you at home can go a long way towards reducing your stress, loneliness and anxiety.
  3. You are responsible for getting up and feeding them, cleaning their hutch and playing with them. This can give you a sense of control, pride and empowerment.
  4. Your fluffy companion can be a calm, comforting presence that keeps you focused on yourself.
